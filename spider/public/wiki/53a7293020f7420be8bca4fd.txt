
<p>The <i><b>IEEE Transactions on Signal Processing</b></i> is a biweekly peer-reviewed scientific journal published by the Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers covering research on signal processing. It was established in 1953 as the <i>IEEE Transactions on Audio</i>, renamed to <i>IEEE Transactions on Audio and Electroacoustics</i> in 1966 and to <i>IEEE Transactions on Acoustics, Speech, and Signal Processing</i> in 1974, before obtaining its current name in 1992. The journal is abstracted and indexed in MEDLINE/PubMed and the Science Citation Index Expanded. According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, the journal has a 2013 impact factor of 3.198. The editor-in-chief is Sergios Theodoridis (National and Kapodistrian University of Athens.</p>
