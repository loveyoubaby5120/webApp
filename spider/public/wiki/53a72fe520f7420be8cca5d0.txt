
<p><i><b>IEEE Transactions on Information Theory</b></i> is a monthly peer-reviewed scientific journal published by the IEEE Information Theory Society. It covers information theory and the mathematics of communications. It was established in 1953 as <i>IRE Transactions on Information Theory</i>. The editor-in-chief is Frank R. Kschischang (University of Toronto). As of 2007, the journal allows the posting of preprints on arXiv.</p>
