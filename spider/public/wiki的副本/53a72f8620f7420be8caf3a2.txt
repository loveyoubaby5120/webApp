<i><b>IEEE Communications Surveys &amp; Tutorials</b></i> is a quarterly peer-reviewed open access academic journal published by the IEEE Communications Society for tutorials and surveys covering all aspects of the communications field. The journal publishes both original articles and reprints of articles featured in other IEEE Communication Society journals. It was established in 1998 and the current editor-in-chief is Ekram Hossain (University of Manitoba).The 2013 impact factor was 6.490.</p>
