<p><i><b>Fuzzy Sets and Systems</b></i> is a peer-reviewed international scientific journal published by Elsevier on behalf of the International Fuzzy Systems Association (IFSA) and was founded in 1978. The editors-in-chief (as of 2010) are Bernard De Baets of the Dept. of Applied Mathematics, Biometrics and Process Control, (at the University of Gent in Belgium), Didier Dubois (of IRIT, Universit&#xE9; Paul Sabatier in Toulouse, France) and Eyke H&#xFC;llermeier (of the Dept. of Mathematics and Computer Science, Universit&#xE4;t Marburg, Germany). The journal publishes 24 issues a year. <i>Fuzzy Sets and Systems</i> is abstracted and indexed by Scopus and the Science Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports released in 2010, its 2-year impact factor calculated for 2009 is 2.138 and its 2010 5-year impact factor for 2009 is 2.551.</p>
