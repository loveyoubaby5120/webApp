<p>The <b>Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition</b> is an annual conference on computer vision and pattern recognition. As ranked by Google Scholar&apos;s h-index metric in 2015, CVPR is the number one venue in Computer Vision and number seven in Engineering and Computer Science. It also has the highest h-index of any conference in any field, is the leading IEEE publications including journals, and it is ranked in the top 70 of all publications. According to Microsoft Academic Search by 2014 there were over 169,936 citations to CVPR papers. It is also highly ranked by various government agencies: It has an &apos;A&apos; rating from the Australian Ranking of ICT Conferences and an &apos;A1&apos; rating from the Brazilian ministry of education(Qualis (2012)).</p>
