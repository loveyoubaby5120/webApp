
<p>The <b>International Conference on Service Oriented Computing</b>, short ICSOC, is an annual conference providing an outstanding forum for academics, industry researchers, developers, and practitioners to report and share groundbreaking work in service-oriented computing. ICSOC has an &apos;A&apos; rating from the Excellence in Research in Australia (ERA) and an &apos;A2&apos; rating from the Brazilian ministry of education. Calls for Papers are regularly published on WikiCFP and on the conference website. The conference is also listed in Elsevier&apos;s Global Events List.</p>
