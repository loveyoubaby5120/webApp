<p>The ACM SIGSOFT <i><b>Software Engineering Notes</b></i> (SEN) is published by the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) for the <i>Special Interest Group on Software Engineering</i> (SIGSOFT). It was established in 1976 and the first issue appeared in May 1976. It provides a forum for informal articles and other information on software engineering. The headquarters is in New York City. Since 1990 it has been published five times a year.</p>
