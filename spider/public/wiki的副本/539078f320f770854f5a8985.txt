<p><i><b>Pattern Recognition Letters</b></i> is a peer-reviewed scientific journal that is published by North Holland, an imprint of Elsevier, on behalf of the International Association for Pattern Recognition. The journal produces 16 issues per year  covering research on pattern recognition. The editors-in-chief are G. Borgefors (Uppsala University), G. Sanniti di Baja (Consiglio Nazionale delle Ricerche, Naples), and S. Sarkar (University of South Florida). According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, the journal has a 2014 impact factor of 1.551.</p>
