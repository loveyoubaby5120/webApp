
<p>The <b>International Conference on Computer-Aided Design</b>, or <b>ICCAD</b>, is a yearly conference about electronic design automation. It is normally held in early November in San Jose, California. It is sponsored by IEEE Circuits and Systems Society, Computer-Aided Design Technical Committee (CANDE), the IEEE Council on Electronic Design Automation (CEDA), SIGDA, and in cooperation with the IEEE Electron Devices Society, and the IEEE Solid State Circuits Society.</p>
