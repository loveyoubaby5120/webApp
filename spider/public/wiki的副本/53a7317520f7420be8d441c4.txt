<p><i><b>IEEE Design &amp; Test of Computers</b></i>, or <i><b>IEEE Design &amp; Test</b></i>, or simply <i><b>Design &amp; Test</b></i>, is a magazine is cosponsored by the Council on EDA, Circuits and Systems Society, and the IEEE Solid State Circuits Society of the IEEE. It is a magazine, rather than a scholarly journal, and hence has articles of general interest to those working in the field, not just research articles.</p>
