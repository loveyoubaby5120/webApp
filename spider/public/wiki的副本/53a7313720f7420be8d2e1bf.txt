<p><i><b>Fundamenta Informaticae</b></i> is a peer-reviewed scientific journal covering computer science. The editor-in-chief is Damian Niwi&#x144;ski. It was established in 1977 by the Polish Mathematical Society as Series IV of the <i>Annales Societatis Mathematicae Polonae</i>, with its main focus on theoretical foundations of computer science. The journal is currently published by IOS Press under the auspices of the European Association for Theoretical Computer Science.</p>
