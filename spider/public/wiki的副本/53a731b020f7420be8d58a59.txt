<p>The <i><b>Journal of Multivariate Analysis</b></i> is a monthly peer-reviewed scientific journal that covers applications and research in the field of multivariate statistical analysis. The journal&apos;s scope includes theoretical results as well as applications of new theoretical methods in the field. Some of the research areas covered include asymptotic theory, Bayesian models, cluster analysis, decision theory, discriminant analysis, distributions, estimation and hypothesis testing, factor analysis, measures of association, multidimensional scaling, multivariate analysis of variance, sequential analysis, time series, and general multivariate methods.</p>
