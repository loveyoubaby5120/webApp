<p>The <i><b>SIAM Journal on Scientific Computing</b></i> (<i><b>SISC</b></i>), formerly <i>SIAM Journal on Scientific &amp; Statistical Computing</i>, is a scientific journal focusing on the research articles on numerical methods and techniques for scientific computation. It is published by the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM). As of August 2012, Hans Petter Langtangen serves as editor-in-chief. The impact factor is around 2.</p>
