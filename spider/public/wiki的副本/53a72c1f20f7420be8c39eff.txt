<p><b>The British Machine Vision Conference (BMVC)</b> is the British Machine Vision Association (BMVA) annual conference on machine vision, image processing, and pattern recognition. It is one of the major international conferences on computer vision and related areas, held in UK. Particularly, BMVC is ranked as A2 by Qualis, and B by ERA. The upcoming 27th BMVC will be held at York University in September 2016.</p>
