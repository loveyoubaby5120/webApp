<p>The <i><b>Journal of Cheminformatics</b></i> is a peer-reviewed open access scientific journal that was established in 2009, published by Chemistry Central. At the end of 2015, the Chemistry Central brand was retired and merged with the SpringerOpen portfolio of open access journals. The editors-in-chief are Rajarchi Guha (NIH Chemical Genomics Center) and Egon Willighagen (Maastricht University). According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, the journal has a 2015 impact factor of 3.949. The most cited paper is on OpenBabel and, as of 2015, has been cited more than 430 times according to the Web of Science.</p>
