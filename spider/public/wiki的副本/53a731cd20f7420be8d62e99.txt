<p><i><b>Data &amp; Knowledge Engineering</b></i> (DKE) is a journal in database systems and knowledge base systems. It is published by Elsevier. It was founded in 1985, and is published monthly. The editor-in-chief is P.P. Chen (Louisiana State University). The journal is indexing in Scopus and Science Citation Index. The 2008 impact factor for the journal was 1.480.</p>
