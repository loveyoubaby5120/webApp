
<p><b>Standard &amp; Poor&apos;s Financial Services LLC</b> (<b>S&amp;P</b>) is an American financial services company. It is a division of S&amp;P Global that publishes financial research and analysis on stocks, bonds and commodities. S&amp;P is known for its stock market indices such as the U.S.-based S&amp;P 500, the Canadian S&amp;P/TSX, and the Australian S&amp;P/ASX 200. S&amp;P is considered one of the Big Three credit-rating agencies, which also include Moody&apos;s Investors Service and Fitch Ratings. Its head office is located on 55 Water Street in Lower Manhattan, New York City.</p>
