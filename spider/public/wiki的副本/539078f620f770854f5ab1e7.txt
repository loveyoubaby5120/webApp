<p><i><b>Algorithmica</b></i> is a monthly peer reviewed, scientific journal, published by Springer Science+Business Media focused on research and application of computer science algorithms. The editor in chief is Ming-Yang Kao. Subject coverage includes sorting, searching, data structures, computational geometry, and linear programming, VLSI, distributed computing, parallel processing, computer aided design, robotics, graphics, data base design, and software tools.</p>
