<p>The <i><b>Journal of Computational Physics</b></i> is a bimonthly scientific journal covering computational physics that was established in 1966 and is published by Elsevier. As of 2015, its editor-in-chief is Remi Abgrall (University of Zurich). According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, the journal has a 2014 impact factor of 2.434.</p>
