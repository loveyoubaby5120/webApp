<p><i><b>Mathematics of Operations Research</b></i> is a peer-reviewed scientific journal first published in February 1976. It focuses on areas of mathematics relevant to the field of operations research such as continuous optimization, discrete optimization, game theory, machine learning, simulation methodology, and stochastic models. The journal is published quarterly by INFORMS (Institute for Operations Research and the Management Sciences), which also publishes other journals including Operations Research (journal) and Management Science (journal). Mathematics of Operations Research is indexed by the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>. In 2014, it has moved to &quot;Issues in Advance&quot; which publishes the articles online as they become available. The H-index of the journal is 50.</p>
