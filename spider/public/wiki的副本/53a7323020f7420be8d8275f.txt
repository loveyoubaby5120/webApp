<p><i><b>IEEE Transactions on Learning Technologies</b></i> is a quarterly peer-reviewed scientific journal covering educational technology and applications. It was established in 2008 and is published by the IEEE Computer Society and IEEE Education Society. The editor-in-chief is Peter Brusilovsky (University of Pittsburgh). According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, the journal has a 2014 impact factor of 1.283.</p>
