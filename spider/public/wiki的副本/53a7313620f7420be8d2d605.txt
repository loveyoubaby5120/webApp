
<p>The <b>Islamic Jihad Union</b> (IJU; Arabic: <span lang="ar" dir="rtl" xml:lang="ar">&#x627;&#x62A;&#x62D;&#x627;&#x62F; &#x627;&#x644;&#x62C;&#x647;&#x627;&#x62F; &#x627;&#x644;&#x625;&#x633;&#x644;&#x627;&#x645;&#x64A;</span>&#x200E;, <small>translit.&#xA0;</small><i><span title="Arabic transliteration" class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none">Itti&#x1E25;ad al-Jihad al-Isl&#x101;m&#x12B;</span></i>&#x200E;) is a militant Islamist organization founded in 2002 as a splinter group of the Islamic Movement of Uzbekistan (IMU). Headquartered in North Waziristan, a mountainous region of northwest Pakistan, bordering Afghanistan, the group has been affiliated with both al-Qaida and the Taliban.</p>
