<p>The <i><b>Journal of Computer-Mediated Communication</b></i> JCMC is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal that covers the interdisciplinary field of computer-mediated communication. It was established in 1994 and is published by Wiley-Blackwell on behalf of the International Communication Association. According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, its 2010 impact factor was 1.958, ranking it 3rd out of 67 journals in the category &quot;Communication&quot; and 13th out of 76 journals in the category &quot;Information Science &amp; Library Science&quot;. By 2014 the ISI Journal Citation Reports &#xA9; Ranking reported JCMC to be 2nd of 76 in the field of Communication, and 4th of 85 in Information Science &amp; Library Science, with an impact factor of 3.117</p>
