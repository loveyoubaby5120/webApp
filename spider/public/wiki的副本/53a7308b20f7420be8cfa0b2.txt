<p>The <i><b>SIAM Journal on Applied Mathematics</b></i> is a peer-reviewed academic journal in applied mathematics published by the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM), with Paul A. Martin as its editor-in-chief. It was founded in 1953 as SIAM&apos;s first journal, the <i>Journal of the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics</i>, and was given its current name in 1966. In most years since 1999, it has been ranked by SCImago Journal Rank as a second-quartile journal in applied mathematics. Together with <i>Communications on Pure and Applied Mathematics</i> it has been called &quot;one of the two greatest American entries in applied math&quot;.</p>
