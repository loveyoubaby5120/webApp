<p><i><b>Medical &amp; Biological Engineering &amp; Computing</b></i> is a monthly peer-reviewed medical journal and an official publication of the International Federation of Medical and Biological Engineering. It is published by Springer Science+Business Media. It covers research in biomedical and clinical engineering. It was established as a bimonthly publication in 1963 under the title <i>Medical Electronics &amp; Biological Engineering</i>. It publishes Original Research Articles, Reviews, and Technical Notes.</p>
