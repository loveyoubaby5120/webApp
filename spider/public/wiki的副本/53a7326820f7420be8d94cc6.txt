<p><b>To be announced</b> (<b>TBA</b>), <b>to be confirmed</b> (<b>TBC</b>), and <b>to be determined</b> (or <b>to be decided</b>, <b>TBD</b>) are placeholder terms used very broadly in event planning to indicate that although something is scheduled or expected to happen, a particular aspect of that remains to be arranged or confirmed.</p>
