<p><i><b>Nucleic Acids Research</b></i> is a peer-reviewed scientific journal published by Oxford University Press. It covers research on nucleic acids, such as DNA and RNA, and related work. According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, the journal&apos;s 2014 impact factor is 9.112. The journal publishes two yearly special issues, one dedicated to biological databases, published in January since 1993, and the other on biological web servers, published in July since 2003.</p>
