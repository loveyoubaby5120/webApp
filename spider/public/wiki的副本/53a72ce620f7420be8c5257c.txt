
<p><b>IEEE Computer Society</b> (sometimes abbreviated <b>Computer Society</b> or <b>CS</b>) is a professional society of IEEE. Its purpose and scope is &quot;to advance the theory, practice, and application of computer and information processing science and technology&quot; and the &quot;professional standing of its members.&quot; The CS is the largest of 39 technical societies organized under the IEEE Technical Activities Board.</p>
