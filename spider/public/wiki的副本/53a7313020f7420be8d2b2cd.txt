<p><i><b>ACM Transactions on Information Systems</b></i>is a quarterly peer-reviewed scientific journal covering research on computer systems and their underlying technology. It was established in 1983 and is published by the Association for Computing Machinery. The editor-in-chief is Maarten de Rijke (University of Amsterdam). The journal is abstracted and indexed in the Science Citation Index Expanded and Current Contents/Engineering, Computing &amp; Technology. According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, the journal has a 2012 impact factor of 1.070.</p>
