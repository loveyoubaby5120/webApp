<p><i><b>IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems</b></i> is a monthly peer-reviewed scientific journal published by the IEEE Computational Intelligence Society. It covers the theory, design, and applications of neural networks and related learning systems. The editor-in-chief is Haibo He (University of Rhode Island). According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, the journal had a 2013 impact factor of 4.370.</p>
