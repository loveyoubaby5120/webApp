<p>The <b>International Symposium on Software Reliability Engineering <i>(</i>IEEE ISSRE</b>) is an academic conference with strong industry participation running since 1990 and covering reliability engineering for software. It is interested in properties such as reliability, availability, safety, security and quality of software. It is sponsored by the IEEE Computer Society. The symposium usually last 4 days and has integrated workshops and tutorials in a multi-track program.</p>
