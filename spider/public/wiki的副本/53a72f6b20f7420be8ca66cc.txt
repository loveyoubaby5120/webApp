<p><i><b>IEEE Transactions on Communications</b></i> is a monthly peer-reviewed scientific journal published by the IEEE Communications Society that focuses on all aspects of telecommunication technology, including telephone, telegraphy, facsimile, and point-to-point television by electromagnetic propagation. The editor-in-chief is Naofal Al-Dhahir (University of Texas at Dallas). According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, the journal has a 2013 impact factor of 1.979.</p>
