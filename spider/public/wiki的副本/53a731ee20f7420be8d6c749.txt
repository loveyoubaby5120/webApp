<p><i><b>Decision Sciences</b></i> is a peer-reviewed academic journal covering research about decision making within the boundaries of an organization, as well as decisions involving inter-firm coordination. According to the 2010 <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, <i>Decision Sciences</i> is ranked 40th out of 140 journals in the category &quot;Management&quot;. <i>Decision Sciences</i> is published by Wiley-Blackwell on behalf of the Decision Sciences Institute. The editor-in-chief is Asoo J. Vakharia.</p>
