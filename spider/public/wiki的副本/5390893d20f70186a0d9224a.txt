<p><i><b>IEEE MultiMedia</b></i> is a quarterly peer-reviewed scientific journal published by the IEEE Computer Society and covering multimedia technologies. Topics of interest include image processing, video processing, audio analysis, text retrieval and understanding, data mining and analysis, and data fusion. It was established in 1994 and the current editor-in-chief is John R. Smith (Thomas J. Watson Research Center). The 2013 impact factor was 1.767.</p>
