<p>The <i><b>Journal of Combinatorial Theory</b></i>, <b>Series A</b> and <b>Series B</b>, are mathematical journals specializing in combinatorics and related areas. They are published by Elsevier. <i>Series A</i> is concerned primarily with structures, designs, and applications of combinatorics. <i>Series B</i> is concerned primarily with graph and matroid theory. The two series are two of the leading journals in the field and are widely known as &quot;JCTA&quot; and &quot;JCTB&quot;.</p>
