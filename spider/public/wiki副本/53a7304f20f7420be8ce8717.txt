
<p>The <i><b>Journal of Management Information Systems</b></i> <i><b>(JMIS)</b></i> is a peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes original research articles in the areas of information systems and information technology. It was established in 1984. The current editor-in-chief of <i>JMIS</i> is Vladimir Zwass. <i>JMIS</i> is published by Taylor &amp; Francis.</p>
