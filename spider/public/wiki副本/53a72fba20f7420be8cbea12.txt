
<p>The <i><b>Proceedings of the IEEE</b></i> is a monthly peer-reviewed scientific journal published by the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE). The journal focuses on electrical engineering and computer science. According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, the journal has a 2011 impact factor of 6.810, ranking it second out of 245 journals in the category &quot;Engineering, Electrical &amp; Electronic&quot;.</p>
