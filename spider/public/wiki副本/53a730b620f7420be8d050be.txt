<p><b>The Association for Logic Programming</b> (ALP) was founded in 1986. Its mission is &quot;to contribute to the development of Logic Programming, relate it to other formal and also to humanistic sciences, and to promote its uses in academia and industry all over the world&quot;. It manages the International Conference on Logic Programming, oversees the <i>Theory and Practice of Logic Programming</i> (TPLP) journal published by Cambridge University Press, and publishes an electronic newsletter.</p>
