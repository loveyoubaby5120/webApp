<p><i><b>Machine Vision and Applications</b></i> is a bimonthly peer-reviewed scientific journal covering image processing. It was established in 1988 and is published by Springer Science+Business Media. The editor-in-chief is Mubarak Shah (University of Central Florida). According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, the journal has a 2012 impact factor of 1.103.</p>
