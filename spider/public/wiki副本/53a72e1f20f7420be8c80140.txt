<p><b>RSS</b> (<b>Rich Site Summary</b>; originally <b>RDF Site Summary</b>; often called <b>Really Simple Syndication</b>) uses a family of standard web feed formats to publish frequently updated information: blog entries, news headlines, audio, video. An RSS document (called &quot;feed&quot;, &quot;web feed&quot;, or &quot;channel&quot;) includes full or summarized text, and metadata, like publishing date and author&apos;s name.</p>
