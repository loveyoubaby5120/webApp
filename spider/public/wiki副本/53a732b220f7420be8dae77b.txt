
<p>The <i><b>Journal of Chemical Information and Modeling</b></i> is a peer-reviewed scientific journal published by the American Chemical Society. It was established in 1961 as the <i>Journal of Chemical Documentation</i>, renamed in 1975 to <i>Journal of Chemical Information and Computer Sciences</i>, and obtained its current name in 2005. The journal covers the fields of chemical informatics and molecular modeling. The editor-in-chief is Kenneth M. Merz Jr. (Michigan State University).</p>
