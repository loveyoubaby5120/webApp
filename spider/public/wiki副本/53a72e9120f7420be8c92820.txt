
<p>The <b>International Conference on Machine Learning</b> (ICML) is the leading international academic conference in machine learning, attracting annually more than 2000 participants from all over the world. It is supported by the International Machine Learning Society (IMLS). The conference attracts leading innovations in the field of machine learning. ICML is a top tier conference, and is one of the two most influential conferences in Machine Learning (along with Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems).</p>
