<p>The <b>International Symposium on Reliable Distributed Systems</b> (<b>SRDS</b>) is an academic conference covering distributed systems design and development, particularly with properties such as reliability, availability, safety, security and real time. The symposium is traditionally a single track event held over three days with a number of associated workshops staged a day before the symposium starts. SRDS is sponsored by the IEEE Computer Society Technical Committee on Distributed Processing.</p>
