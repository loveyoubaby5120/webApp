
<p><i><b>Combinatorica</b></i> is an international journal of mathematics, publishing papers in the fields of combinatorics and computer science. It started in 1981, with L&#xE1;szl&#xF3; Babai and L&#xE1;szl&#xF3; Lov&#xE1;sz as the editors-in-chief with Paul Erd&#x151;s as honorary editor-in-chief. The current editors-in-chief are L&#xE1;szl&#xF3; Babai, L&#xE1;szl&#xF3; Lov&#xE1;sz, and Alexander Schrijver. The advisory board consists of Ronald Graham, Andr&#xE1;s Hajnal, Gyula O. H. Katona, Mikl&#xF3;s Simonovits, and Vera S&#xF3;s. It is published by the J&#xE1;nos Bolyai Mathematical Society and Springer Verlag. The following members of the <i>Hungarian School of Combinatorics</i> have strongly contributed to the journal as authors, or have served as editors: Mikl&#xF3;s Ajtai, J&#xF3;zsef Beck, Andr&#xE1;s Frank, P&#xE9;ter Frankl, Zolt&#xE1;n F&#xFC;redi, Andr&#xE1;s Hajnal, Gyula Katona, L&#xE1;szl&#xF3; Pyber, Mikl&#xF3;s Simonovits, Vera S&#xF3;s, Endre Szemer&#xE9;di, Tam&#xE1;s Sz&#x151;nyi, &#xC9;va Tardos, G&#xE1;bor Tardos.</p>
