<p>The <i><b>Journal of Computer and System Sciences</b></i> (JCSS) is a peer-reviewed scientific journal in the field of computer science. <i>JCSS</i> is published by Elsevier, and it was started in 1967. Many influential scientific articles have been published in <i>JCSS</i>; these include five papers that have won the G&#xF6;del Prize. Its managing editor is Michael Segal.</p>
