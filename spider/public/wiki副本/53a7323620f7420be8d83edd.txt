
<p>The <i><b>Journal of Visual Communication and Image Representation</b></i> is a peer-reviewed academic journal of media studies published by Elsevier. It was established in 1990 and is published in 8 issues per year. The editors-in-chief are M.T. Sun (University of Washington) and Z. Liu (Microsoft Research). According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, the journal has a 2015 impact factor of 1.530.</p>
