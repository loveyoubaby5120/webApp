
<p>An <b>anti-aircraft vehicle</b>, also known as a <b>self-propelled anti-aircraft gun</b> (<b>SPAAG</b>) or <b>self-propelled air defense system</b> (<b>SPAD</b>), is a mobile vehicle with a dedicated anti-aircraft capability. The Russian equivalent of <i>SPAAG</i> is <i>ZSU</i>, for <i>zenitnaya samokhodnaya ustanovka</i>, (&quot;anti-aircraft self-propelled mount&quot;).</p>
