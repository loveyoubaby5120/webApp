<p>The <i><b>Journal of Computational Biology</b></i> is a monthly peer-reviewed scientific journal covering computational biology and bioinformatics. It was established in 1994 and is published by Mary Ann Liebert, Inc. The editors-in-chief are Sorin Istrail (Brown University) and Michael S. Waterman (University of Southern California). According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, the journal has a 2012 impact factor of 1.564.</p>
