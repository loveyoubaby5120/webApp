
<p>The <i><b>Journal of the Association for Information Systems</b></i> is a monthly peer-reviewed scientific journal that covers research in the areas of information systems and technology. It is an official journal of the Association for Information Systems and is published electronically. The journal was established in 2000 and is abstracted and indexed in Science Citation Index Expanded, Social Sciences Citation Index, and Current Contents/Social &amp; Behavioral Sciences. According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, the journal has a 2015 impact factor of 1.774.</p>
