
<p>The <b>ACM Symposium on User Interface Software and Technology</b> (<b>UIST</b>) is an annual conference for technical innovations in human&#x2013;computer interfaces. UIST is sponsored by ACM SIGCHI and ACM SIGGRAPH. By impact factor, it is the most impactful conference in the field of human&#x2013;computer interaction. Scott Hudson is the current chair of the UIST community, which organizes the UIST conference.</p>
