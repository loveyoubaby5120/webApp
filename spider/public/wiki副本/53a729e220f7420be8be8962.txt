
<p>The <b>Extended Semantic Web Conference</b> (abbreviated as <b>ESWC</b>), formerly known as the <b>European Semantic Web Conference</b>, is a yearly international academic conference on the topic of the Semantic Web. The event began in 2004 as the European Semantic Web Symposium. The goal of the event is &quot;to bring together researchers and practitioners dealing with different aspects of semantics on the Web&quot;.</p>
