
<p>The <i><b>European Journal of Operational Research</b></i> (EJOR) is a peer-reviewed academic journal in operations research. It was founded in 1977 by the Association of European Operational Research Societies, and is published by Elsevier, with Roman S&#x142;owi&#x144;ski as its editor-in-chief. Currently, it publishes at a rate of 24 issues per annual volume, with approximately 250 pages per issue.</p>
