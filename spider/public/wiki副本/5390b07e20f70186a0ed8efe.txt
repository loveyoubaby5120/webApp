<p><i><b>Transportation Science</b></i> is a peer-reviewed academic journal published by the Institute for Operations Research and the Management Sciences (INFORMS). The studies published in the journal apply operations research techniques to problems in the full range of transportation sectors, including air travel, rail transport, commuter lines, and vehicular travel/traffic. From time to time, studies published in <i>Transportation Science</i> are featured in mainstream publications.</p>
