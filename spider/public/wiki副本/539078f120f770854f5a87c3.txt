
<p><i><b>SIAM Journal on Discrete Mathematics</b></i> is a peer-reviewed mathematics journal published quarterly by the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM). The journal includes articles on pure and applied discrete mathematics. It was established in 1988, along with the <i>SIAM Journal on Matrix Analysis and Applications</i>, to replace the <i>SIAM Journal on Algebraic and Discrete Methods</i>. The journal is indexed by <i>Mathematical Reviews</i> and Zentralblatt MATH. Its 2009 MCQ was 0.57, and its 2009 impact factor was 0.668. In 2015 the later figure had risen to 0.972.</p>
