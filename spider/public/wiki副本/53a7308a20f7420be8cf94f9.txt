<p><i><b>Language and Linguistics Compass</b></i> is an online peer-reviewed linguistics journal established by Blackwell Publishers (now Wiley-Blackwell) in 2006. One of eight Compass journals, <i>Language and Linguistics Compass</i> publishes state-of-the-art reviews review articles aimed at an international readership. The target audience includes academic researchers, postgraduates students and advanced undergraduates.</p>
