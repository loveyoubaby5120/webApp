
<p>The <b>International Semantic Web Conference</b> (<b>ISWC</b>) is a series of academic conferences and the premier international forum, for the Semantic Web / Linked Data Community. Here, scientists, industry specialists, and practitioners meet to discuss the future of practical, scalable, user-friendly, and game changing solutions. Its proceedings are published in the Lecture Notes in Computer Science by Springer-Verlag.</p>
