<p>The <b>Institute for Operations Research and the Management Sciences</b> (<b>INFORMS</b>) is an international society for practitioners in the fields of operations research (OR) and management science. It was established in 1995 with the merger of the <b>Operations Research Society of America</b> (ORSA) and <b>The Institute of Management Sciences</b> (TIMS). The 2016 president of the institute is Professor Edward H. Kaplan of Yale. L. Robin Keller served as president in 2015.</p>
