<p>The <i><b>Journal of Grid Computing</b></i> is a peer-reviewed scientific journal published by Springer Science+Business Media. According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, the journal has a 2010 impact factor of 1.556. The editors-in-chief are P&#xE9;ter Kacsuk (MTA SZTAKI Computer and Automation Research Institute, Budapest, Hungary, and the University of Westminster) and Ian Foster (Argonne National Laboratory and the University of Chicago, Illinois, USA)</p>
