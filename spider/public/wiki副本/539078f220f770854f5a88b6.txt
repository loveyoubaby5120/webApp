
<p><i><b>IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing</b></i> is a bimonthly peer-reviewed scientific journal covering technology related to the mobility of users, systems, data and computing. It was established in 2002 and is published by the IEEE Computer Society. The editor-in-chief is Prasant Mohapatra (University of California, Davis). According to the <i>Journal Citation Reports</i>, the journal has a 2013 impact factor of 2.912.</p>
