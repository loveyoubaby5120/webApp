<p>The <b>European Space Agency</b> (<b>ESA</b>; French: <span lang="fr"><i><b>Agence spatiale europ&#xE9;enne</b></i></span>, <b>ASE</b>) is an intergovernmental organisation dedicated to the exploration of space, with 22 member states. Established in 1975 and headquartered in Paris, France, ESA has a worldwide staff of about 2,000 and an annual budget of about &#x20AC;5.25 billion / US$5.77 billion (2016).</p>
